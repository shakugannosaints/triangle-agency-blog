# Welcome to Triangle Agency Blog

Welcome to the official Triangle Agency Blog! This is a community-driven space dedicated to exploring, discussing, and sharing knowledge about the **Triangle Agency** tabletop role-playing game.

## What is Triangle Agency?

Triangle Agency is a tabletop role-playing game where players take on the roles of operatives working for a mysterious organization. The game is known for its unique mechanics, immersive storytelling, and the enigmatic nature of the organization itself.

## What You'll Find Here

This blog serves as a comprehensive resource for Triangle Agency enthusiasts, covering:

- **Game Mechanics**: Deep dives into the rules, systems, and mechanics that make Triangle Agency unique
- **Campaign Stories**: Shared experiences and narratives from GMs and players
- **Character Guides**: Tips for creating compelling characters and developing their stories
- **World Building**: Insights into the lore, organizations, and mysteries within the Triangle Agency universe
- **Community Highlights**: Featuring stories and experiences from the community
